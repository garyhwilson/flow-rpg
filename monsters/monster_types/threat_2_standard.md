# THREAT LEVEL 2: STANDARD ENEMIES

Standard enemies are worthy opponents that create balanced fights. They have enough resources to be interesting without overwhelming the party.

## Design Notes

**Role:** Core opponents, balanced encounters

**Usage:**
- Appear in pairs or small groups (2-4)
- Can solo weak parties
- Create tactical challenges
- Require coordination to defeat

**Flow Impact:**
- Start with 2-3 Flow
- Can use 1-2 abilities per fight
- Generate +1 Flow when defeated

---

## ORC WARRIOR (Threat 2)
*Medium humanoid (orc), chaotic*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +3  **Mind:** -1  **Awareness:** +1  **Will:** +2  **Presence:** +0

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 12
**Guard:** 12 (7 + Body +3 + chainmail +2)
**Flow:** Starts with 3 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Greataxe** - 2d6 + 3, deals 1d6+3 damage
Range: Close

### ABILITIES
**Aggressive Charge** (Cost: 1 Flow)
Move to target and attack with +2 damage.

**Savage Resilience** (Cost: 2 Flow)
When reduced below half Vitality, gain +2 to all rolls for scene.

### TACTICS
Aggressive, charge into melee. Use Savage Resilience when bloodied. Fight to near-death before retreating.

## DIRE WOLF (Threat 2)
*Large beast, unaligned*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +3  **Mind:** -2  **Awareness:** +3  **Will:** +1  **Presence:** -1

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 10
**Guard:** 10 (7 + Body +3)
**Flow:** Starts with 2 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Bite** - 2d6 + 3, deals 1d6+3 damage
Range: Close
Special: On 10+, target knocked prone

### ABILITIES
**Pack Tactics** (Passive)
+2 to attack when ally adjacent to target.

**Trip and Tear** (Cost: 2 Flow)
Knock target prone and make immediate second bite attack.

### TACTICS
Use pack tactics, coordinate with allies. Target isolated enemies. Trip and Tear when able.

---

## GOBLIN SHAMAN (Threat 2)
*Small humanoid (goblinoid), chaotic*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** -1  **Mind:** +1  **Awareness:** +3  **Will:** +1  **Presence:** +0

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 8
**Guard:** 8 (7 + Body -1 + Mage Armor +2)
**Flow:** Starts with 3 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Staff** - 2d6 - 1, deals 1d6 damage (rarely used)
Range: Close

**Spells** - Casts with Awareness
Known Spells: Spark, Bolt, Mage Armor, Heal

### ABILITIES
**Spellcasting** (Passive)
Can cast spells. Prefers Bolt (-1 Flow) for attacks.

**Heal Allies** (Cost: 3 Flow)
Heal spell on wounded ally. Awareness + d6 Vitality restored.

### TACTICS
Cast Mage Armor at start. Use Bolt from range. Heal strongest ally if available. Hide behind minions.

---

## ANIMATED ARMOR (Threat 2)
*Medium construct, unaligned*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +4  **Mind:** -3  **Awareness:** +0  **Will:** +3  **Presence:** -2

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 14
**Guard:** 13 (7 + Body +4 + heavy armor +2)
**Flow:** Starts with 2 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Sword Slam** - 2d6 + 4, deals 1d6+4 damage
Range: Close

### ABILITIES
**Construct Nature** (Passive)
Immune to mind-affecting, poison, disease. Mindless.

**Shield Bash** (Cost: 1 Flow)
Attack with shield. On hit, target pushed back to Near range.

**Relentless** (Cost: 2 Flow)
When reduced to 0 Vitality, instead drop to 1 Vitality (once per encounter).

### TACTICS
Guard objectives mindlessly. Never retreat. Use Shield Bash to protect area. Relentless when "killed."

---

*Standard enemies form the core of most encounters. Mix with minions for tactical depth or use in pairs for balanced challenges.*
