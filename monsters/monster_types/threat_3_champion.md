# THREAT LEVEL 3: CHAMPION ENEMIES

Champion enemies are serious threats that can challenge an entire party. They have significant resources, powerful abilities, and tactical intelligence that makes them formidable centerpieces of encounters.

## Design Notes

**Role:** Serious threats, encounter centerpieces, resource drains

**Usage:**
- Usually solo or with minion support (1 champion + 3-6 minions)
- Can challenge full party alone
- Create multi-round tactical puzzles
- Force coordinated party responses

**Flow Impact:**
- Start with 4-6 Flow
- Can use multiple abilities per fight
- Generate +1 Flow when defeated (same as others)
- Force players to spend their accumulated Flow

## OGRE BRUTE (Threat 3)
*Large giant, chaotic*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +6  **Mind:** -2  **Awareness:** +1  **Will:** +2  **Presence:** -1

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 18
**Guard:** 11 (7 + Body +6 + slow -2)
**Flow:** Starts with 4 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Greatclub** - 2d6 + 6, deals 2d6+6 damage
Range: Close
Special: Can hit multiple adjacent targets

**Boulder Throw** - 2d6 + 6, deals 2d6+4 damage
Range: Far
Special: On 10+, target knocked prone

### ABILITIES
**Sweeping Strike** (Cost: 2 Flow)
Attack all adjacent enemies at once. Separate roll for each target.

**Thick Skull** (Cost: 1 Flow, Reaction)
When hit by attack, reduce damage by 1d6.

**Regeneration** (Passive)
Recovers 2 Vitality at start of each round while above 0 Vitality.

### TACTICS
Simple but effective. Charge into melee, use Sweeping Strike against clustered enemies. Throw boulders at ranged attackers. Thick Skull against big hits. Too dumb to retreat—fights to death.

## BATTLE MAGE (Threat 3)
*Medium humanoid (any), any alignment*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +1  **Mind:** +4  **Awareness:** +2  **Will:** +3  **Presence:** +1

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 14
**Guard:** 12 (7 + Body +1 + Mage Armor +4)
**Flow:** Starts with 6 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Staff** - 2d6 + 1, deals 1d6+1 damage (rarely used)
Range: Close

**Spells** - Casts with Mind (+4)
Known Spells: Spark, Bolt, Fireball, Mage Armor, Shield, Counterspell, Fly

### ABILITIES
**War Caster** (Passive)
Can cast spells while threatened in melee without penalty. Maintains concentration easily.

**Counterspell** (Cost: 3 Flow, Reaction)
When enemy casts spell, attempt to counter it. Roll Mind vs. enemy's casting roll. On success, spell fails.

**Spell Barrage** (Cost: 4 Flow)
Cast two spells in one action. Both must target different enemies or areas.

### TACTICS
Cast Mage Armor at start. Use Fly to gain positional advantage. Fireball clustered enemies. Bolt priority targets. Counterspell enemy spellcasters. Use Spell Barrage when party is grouped. Maintain distance—flee melee if possible.

## YOUNG BLACK DRAGON (Threat 3)
*Large dragon, chaotic evil*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +5  **Mind:** +2  **Awareness:** +4  **Will:** +4  **Presence:** +3

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 20
**Guard:** 14 (7 + Body +5 + scales +2)
**Flow:** Starts with 5 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Bite** - 2d6 + 5, deals 2d6+5 damage
Range: Close
Special: On 10+, grapple target (requires Awareness check to escape)

**Claw** - 2d6 + 5, deals 1d6+5 damage
Range: Close
Special: Can attack twice with claws instead of bite

**Acid Breath** (Recharge: 5-6 on d6, rolled at start of each round)
All creatures in Close cone range. 2d6 + 4 damage (Awareness save for half).
Special: Ignores armor. Costs 0 Flow (natural ability).

### ABILITIES
**Dragon Fear** (Cost: 2 Flow)
All enemies in Near range must make Will save (DC 12 + Presence). On fail, -2 to all actions for scene.

**Amphibious** (Passive)
Breathe air and water. Swim speed equal to ground speed. Prefers swamps and marshes.

**Flyby** (Cost: 1 Flow)
Fly past enemy, make claw attack, continue flying without provoking reactions.

### TACTICS
Open with Dragon Fear. Use mobility—fly, swim, circle enemies. Acid Breath on cooldown against groups. Bite and grapple dangerous melee fighters. Flyby against ranged attackers. Intelligent—retreat if reduced below half Vitality to lair or ambush position.

## VAMPIRE SPAWN (Threat 3)
*Medium undead, neutral evil*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +4  **Mind:** +1  **Awareness:** +3  **Will:** +2  **Presence:** +2

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 16
**Guard:** 13 (7 + Body +4 + unnatural speed +2)
**Flow:** Starts with 5 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Claw** - 2d6 + 4, deals 1d6+4 damage
Range: Close
Special: Can attack twice with claws

**Bite** - 2d6 + 4, deals 1d6+6 damage
Range: Close (must have target grappled)
Special: Heals vampire for damage dealt

### ABILITIES
**Blood Drain** (Cost: 2 Flow)
Grapple target, then bite. If bite hits, deal damage and heal for amount dealt. Target's max Vitality reduced by amount until long rest.

**Spider Climb** (Passive)
Climb walls and ceilings at full speed. Fight from any surface.

**Regeneration** (Passive)
Recover 3 Vitality at start of each round while above 0 Vitality. Doesn't work if took radiant damage or in sunlight.

**Vulnerabilities** (Passive)
Sunlight: Can't regenerate, disadvantage on all rolls.
Running water: Can't cross without taking 2d6 damage.
Wooden stake through heart while helpless: Instant death.

### TACTICS
Use Spider Climb for ambush from above. Focus on isolating single target. Grapple and Blood Drain priority target. Use walls and ceilings for tactical advantage. If seriously wounded, flee to regenerate. Intelligent—knows weaknesses, avoids sunlight and holy symbols.

## STONE GOLEM (Threat 3)
*Large construct, unaligned*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +7  **Mind:** -4  **Awareness:** +0  **Will:** +5  **Presence:** -3

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 20
**Guard:** 16 (7 + Body +7 + stone armor +2)
**Flow:** Starts with 4 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Slam** - 2d6 + 7, deals 2d6+7 damage
Range: Close
Special: On 10+, target knocked back to Near range

### ABILITIES
**Construct Nature** (Passive)
Immune to poison, psychic, mind-affecting, disease. Mindless—follows orders literally.

**Stone Resilience** (Passive)
Resistant to non-magical weapons (half damage). Immune to most spells (magic resistance).

**Slow** (Curse, Cost: 3 Flow)
Touch attack. Target must save (Will DC 13) or be slowed—half movement, -2 to all rolls, can't take reactions. Lasts until dispelled.

**Earthquake Stomp** (Cost: 3 Flow)
All creatures within Close range must save (Body DC 14) or fall prone. Difficult terrain created in area.

### ABILITIES
**Magic Immunity** (Passive)
Immune to most spells. Only specific spells work:
- Transmute rock to mud (slows it)
- Stone to flesh (removes resistances for 1 round)
- Earthquake (damages it)

### TACTICS
Mindlessly guard location or follow orders. Never retreat. Slam enemies in reach. Use Earthquake Stomp when multiple enemies are close. Use Slow on most dangerous melee fighter. Prioritize targets by threat level if intelligent controller directs it.

## WEREWOLF ALPHA (Threat 3)
*Medium shapechanger (humanoid/beast), chaotic*

### ATTRIBUTES
**Body:** +4  **Mind:** +0  **Awareness:** +3  **Will:** +2  **Presence:** +1

### DEFENSES
**Vitality:** 16
**Guard:** 12 (7 + Body +4 + agility +1)
**Flow:** Starts with 5 Flow

### OFFENSE
**Bite** - 2d6 + 4, deals 2d6+4 damage
Range: Close
Special: On 10+, target contracts lycanthropy (save to resist)

**Claws** - 2d6 + 4, deals 1d6+4 damage
Range: Close
Special: Can attack twice with claws

### ABILITIES
**Shapechanger** (Cost: 1 Flow)
Switch between human, hybrid, and wolf form. Each form has advantages:
- Human: Blend in, use tools and weapons
- Hybrid: Combat stats (default)
- Wolf: Tracking, speed, intimidation

**Pack Leader** (Passive)
Allied wolves (dire or regular) and werewolves within Near range gain +1 to all rolls.

**Infectious Bite** (Cost: 2 Flow)
Bite attack. On hit, target must make Will save (DC 13) or contract lycanthropy. Full moon triggers transformation.

**Regeneration** (Passive)
Recover 2 Vitality at start of each round while above 0 Vitality. Silver weapons prevent this regeneration.

### VULNERABILITIES
**Silver Weakness** (Passive)
Silver weapons ignore damage resistance and prevent regeneration.

**Damage Resistance** (Passive)
Resistant to non-magical, non-silver weapons (half damage).

### TACTICS
Lead pack of wolves/werewolves. Use Pack Leader to buff allies. Focus on spreading lycanthropy—bite multiple targets. Use shapeshifting tactically (human to infiltrate, wolf to pursue, hybrid to fight). Retreat if taking silver damage and below half Vitality. Intelligent tactician.

---

*Champion enemies are formidable opponents that require coordination and resource expenditure to defeat. Use them as encounter centerpieces, often supported by minions to create complex tactical situations.*
