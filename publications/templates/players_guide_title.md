### About Flow RPG

Flow RPG features a revolutionary flexible casting system where the same spell creates different effects based on your approach. Combined with a unified Flow economy and calling-driven mechanics, it creates deep tactical gameplay that serves the narrative.

**Core Innovations:**
- **Flexible Casting:** Cast with Mind, Awareness, Will, or Presence
- **Flow Economy:** One resource powers everything
- **Callings:** Your motivation provides mechanical benefits
- **Dynamic Stances:** Tactical combat choices
