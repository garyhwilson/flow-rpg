### About Flow RPG for GMs

As Game Master, you'll leverage the Flow economy to create dynamic pacing and dramatic tension. The flexible casting system means every spellcaster plays differently, and the calling system ensures characters have built-in story hooks and mechanical rewards.

**GM Tools:**
- **Flow Management:** Control encounter difficulty and pacing
- **Calling Integration:** Drive story through character motivations
- **Flexible Magic:** Support creative spellcasting approaches
- **Balanced Systems:** Understanding how everything fits together

**Key Principle:** The Flow economy is your primary tool for creating engaging challenges and managing pace. Master it, and you'll run great games.
