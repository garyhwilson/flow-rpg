### System
Flow RPG uses a unified Flow economy to power combat, magic, and narrative momentum. Every action feeds into a dynamic resource system that creates dramatic pacing and tactical depth.

### Core Concepts
- **Flexible Casting:** Same spell, different effects based on your approach
- **Callings System:** Character motivation drives mechanical benefits
- **Flow Economy:** One resource unifies all subsystems
- **Stance System:** Dynamic combat with meaningful positioning
