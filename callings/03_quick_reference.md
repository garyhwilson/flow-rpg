## Calling Conflicts & Alliances

### Common Conflicts

- **Advocate vs. Mediator**: Justice vs. harmony
- **Guardian vs. Champion**: Support vs. control
- **Exemplar vs. Seeker**: External achievement vs. authentic self-expression
- **Scholar vs. Enthusiast**: Analysis vs. experience
- **Sentinel vs. Champion**: Caution vs. decisive action

### Natural Alliances

- **Advocate + Scholar**: Truth and justice
- **Guardian + Mediator**: Caring and peace
- **Exemplar + Champion**: Achievement and strength
- **Seeker + Enthusiast**: Authenticity and exploration
- **Sentinel + Guardian**: Protection and care

## Calling Growth & Stress Patterns

### Stress Directions

When under extreme pressure, characters may exhibit unhealthy aspects:

- **Advocate → Exemplar**: Becomes obsessed with being seen as perfect
- **Guardian → Champion**: Becomes controlling and domineering
- **Exemplar → Mediator**: Becomes apathetic and disengaged
- **Seeker → Guardian**: Becomes clingy and dependent
- **Scholar → Enthusiast**: Becomes scattered and hyperactive
- **Sentinel → Exemplar**: Becomes competitive and status-focused
- **Enthusiast → Advocate**: Becomes critical and perfectionistic
- **Champion → Scholar**: Retreats into isolation and planning
- **Mediator → Sentinel**: Becomes anxious and reactive

### Growth Directions

When secure and growing, characters integrate positive aspects:

- **Advocate → Enthusiast**: Learns joy and spontaneity
- **Guardian → Seeker**: Develops authentic self-expression
- **Exemplar → Sentinel**: Gains loyalty and teamwork
- **Seeker → Advocate**: Finds principles worth fighting for
- **Scholar → Champion**: Develops confidence and action
- **Sentinel → Mediator**: Finds inner peace and trust
- **Enthusiast → Scholar**: Gains depth and focus
- **Champion → Guardian**: Learns compassion and service
- **Mediator → Exemplar**: Develops goals and initiative
