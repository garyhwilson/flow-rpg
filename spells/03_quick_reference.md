# SPELLS - QUICK REFERENCE

## Casting Basics

**Casting Roll:** 2d6 + [Chosen Attribute] + Sorcery

**Attributes:**

- **Mind:** Precision, control, calculation
- **Awareness:** Maximum effect, sensing, instinct
- **Will:** Negative Momentum casting, persistence, force
- **Presence:** Social effects, fear/inspiration, drama

**Success Thresholds:**

- **6 or less:** Failure (generates +1 enemy Momentum)
- **7-9:** Partial success
- **10+:** Full success

**Armor Penalties:**

- Light: -1 Sorcery
- Medium: -2 Sorcery
- Heavy: Cannot cast

## Spell Details by Tier

### CANTRIPS (0 MOMENTUM)

#### Spark - Basic magical attack

- **Mind:** 1d6, precise targeting
- **Awareness:** 1d6, +1 on 10+
- **Will:** 1d6, cast at negative Momentum
- **Presence:** 1d6, target -1 next action

#### Light - Illuminate area

- **Mind:** Control brightness/patterns
- **Awareness:** Reveals hidden things
- **Will:** Works in magical darkness
- **Presence:** Dramatic effects, +1 Perform

#### Mage Armor - Protective barrier

- **Mind:** +2 Guard, absorb 3 damage
- **Awareness:** +2 Guard, absorb 2, alerts to attacks
- **Will:** +2 Guard, absorb 2, maintains through disruption
- **Presence:** +2 Guard, absorb 2, +1 social rolls

_Doesn't stack with armor, breaks after absorbing damage, can recast_

---

### STANDARD SPELLS (-1 MOMENTUM)

#### Bolt - Ranged attack

- **Mind:** Mind + d6, ignores cover
- **Awareness:** Awareness + d6, seeks weak points
- **Will:** Will + d6, cast at negative Momentum
- **Presence:** Presence + d6, causes fear

#### Shield - Protective ward

- **Mind:** +Mind Guard, calculated angles
- **Awareness:** +Awareness Guard, warns of attacks
- **Will:** +Will Guard, maintains when unconscious
- **Presence:** +Presence Guard, demoralizes attackers

_Lasts scene, can target others, stacks with armor_

#### Charm - Influence mind

- **Mind:** Actions seem logical, +Mind Social
- **Awareness:** Sense desires, +Awareness Social
- **Will:** Dominate weak-willed (if Will higher)
- **Presence:** Irresistible charisma, +Presence Social

_Scene duration, social spell_

#### Blur - Defensive obscurement

- **Mind:** +2 defense, probability manipulation
- **Awareness:** +2 defense, dodge before hit
- **Will:** +2 defense, works when stunned
- **Presence:** +2 defense, mesmerizing movements

_Self only, scene duration_

---

### ADVANCED SPELLS (-3 MOMENTUM)

#### Fireball - Area damage

- **Mind:** 2d6 + Mind to zone, exclude X targets (X = Mind)
- **Awareness:** 2d6 + Awareness to zone, maximum damage
- **Will:** 2d6 + Will to zone, cast at negative Momentum
- **Presence:** 2d6 + Presence to zone, survivors flee

#### Heal - Restore health

- **Mind:** Mind + d6 Vitality, diagnose injuries
- **Awareness:** Awareness + d6 Vitality, sense what needs healing
- **Will:** Will + d6 Vitality, cast at negative Momentum/when dying
- **Presence:** Presence + d6 Vitality, +1 next action

_Touch range_

#### Invisibility - Concealment

- **Mind:** Mind + 3 Stealth, perfect (no shimmer)
- **Awareness:** Awareness + 3 Stealth, sense when observed
- **Will:** Will + 3 Stealth, maintain when attacking
- **Presence:** Presence + 3 Stealth, people ignore you

_Scene duration, can target others_

#### Dispel - Counter magic

- **Mind:** Mind + Sorcery vs spell, calculated unweaving
- **Awareness:** Awareness + Sorcery vs spell, sense threads
- **Will:** Will + Sorcery vs spell, brute force
- **Presence:** Presence + Sorcery vs spell, +bonus if know caster's name

_Can counter spells or remove ongoing effects_

---

### MASTER SPELLS (-5 MOMENTUM)

#### Lightning Storm - Massive area attack

- **Mind:** 3d6 to chosen targets (multiple zones)
- **Awareness:** 3d6 to all enemies in sight
- **Will:** 3d6 to zones, maintains for scene
- **Presence:** 3d6 to zones, breaks morale

#### Teleport - Instant movement

- **Mind:** Anywhere you can calculate coordinates
- **Awareness:** Anywhere you've been/can sense
- **Will:** Can bring unwilling targets (opposed Will)
- **Presence:** Dramatic timing, +3 next Social action

_Can bring passengers (# = Attribute)_

#### Dominate - Control actions

- **Mind:** Commands seem reasonable to target
- **Awareness:** Sense/use target's skills/knowledge
- **Will:** Absolute control if Will exceeds theirs
- **Presence:** Target believes they want to serve you

_Near range, scene duration (may persist)_

#### Time Stop - Pause time

- **Mind:** Take 3 turns
- **Awareness:** React to everything, interrupt any action
- **Will:** Extend duration through concentration
- **Presence:** Dramatic monologue, guaranteed social impact

_Can position, buff, prepare—can't directly harm frozen creatures_

## Attribute Quick Reference

### MIND + Sorcery (The Wizard's Way)

**Strengths:** Precision, control, calculation, avoiding allies
**Best For:** Tactical combat, complex situations, avoiding collateral damage
**Spells:** Bolt (ignores cover), Fireball (exclude allies), Invisibility (perfect)

### AWARENESS + Sorcery (The Sorcerer's Way)

**Strengths:** Maximum damage, sensing, instinct, detecting
**Best For:** Pure damage, information-gathering, reactive magic
**Spells:** Bolt (maximum damage), Charm (sense desires), Heal (emergency)

### WILL + Sorcery (The Divine/Pact Way)

**Strengths:** Negative Momentum casting, persistence, force, reliability
**Best For:** Reliability under pressure, depleted resources, concentration
**Spells:** Any spell at negative Momentum, Shield (persists when unconscious), Invisibility (maintain when attacking)

### PRESENCE + Sorcery (The Bard's Way)

**Strengths:** Social effects, fear/inspiration, drama, group influence
**Best For:** Social encounters, intimidation, inspiration, performance
**Spells:** Charm (irresistible), Fireball (terrifying), Teleport (dramatic)

## Magic Access & Requirements

**Minimum Requirement:** Mind + Awareness ≥ +2

**Mage Sight (All Casters):**

- Detect magic in Near zone (out of combat)
- Use in Defensive Stance instead of building Momentum
- Awareness +3: Identify spell types/signatures

## Common Spell Combinations

### Defensive Setup

1. Mage Armor (0 Momentum) - +2 Guard, absorb damage
2. Shield on self (-1 Momentum) - +Attribute Guard
3. Blur (-1 Momentum) - +2 defense
   **Total:** -2 Momentum for layered defense

### Aggressive Offense

1. Aggressive Stance
2. Bolt repeatedly (-1 Momentum each)
3. Generate Momentum from successful attacks
4. Build to Fireball (-3 Momentum)

### Support Role

1. Shield on allies (-1 Momentum each)
2. Defensive Stance for Momentum
3. Heal when needed (-3 Momentum)
4. Maintain protection

### Social Control

1. Charm at scene start (-1 Momentum)
2. Leverage bonus throughout scene
3. Different attributes for different approaches

## Momentum Management Guide

### Starting Momentum by Rank

- **Novice:** 3-5 Momentum
- **Seasoned:** 5-7 Momentum
- **Veteran+:** 7-10 Momentum

### Spending Priorities

**At 1-2 Momentum:**

- Cantrips + one standard spell
- Or save for advanced spell

**At 3-4 Momentum:**

- One advanced spell, or
- Multiple standard spells, or
- Mix standard + cantrips

**At 5+ Momentum:**

- Master spell available
- Or multiple advanced spells
- Strategic choice

### Building Momentum

- **Aggressive Stance:** +1 Momentum per successful attack
- **Defensive Stance:** +1 Momentum per turn (or Mage Sight)
- **Cantrips:** Free attacks that might generate Momentum

## Tactical Spell Selection

### Combat Scenarios

**Multiple Weak Enemies:**

- Fireball (-3 Momentum) for groups
- Lightning Storm (-5 Momentum) for armies

**Single Strong Enemy:**

- Bolt (-1 Momentum) repeatedly
- Build to advanced/master spell

**Allies in Melee:**

- Mind-cast spells (exclude allies)
- Single-target spells (Bolt, Heal)
- Shield to protect them

**Overwhelmed:**

- Defensive buffs (Mage Armor, Shield, Blur)
- Escape (Invisibility, Teleport)
- Control (Charm, Dominate)

### Social Scenarios

**Negotiation:**

- Charm (Mind or Presence)
- Display of power (Bolt, Light)

**Investigation:**

- Charm (Awareness - sense desires)
- Mage Sight
- Mind-cast diagnostic spells

**Infiltration:**

- Invisibility (Mind for perfect)
- Light for exploration
- Teleport for access

**Intimidation:**

- Presence-cast anything
- Display of power
- Reputation from past spells

## Spell Failure

**When you roll 6 or less:**

- Spell fails
- Generate +1 Momentum for enemy
- Lose Momentum anyway (paid the cost)

**Failure Manifestations:**

- **Mind:** Miscalculation, minor paradox
- **Awareness:** Magical backlash, sense overload
- **Will:** Power source rejection
- **Presence:** Reality refuses, embarrassing

## GM Quick Rules

**Setting Dispel DCs:**

- Standard Spell: 9
- Advanced Spell: 12
- Master Spell: 15
- Or opposed roll if countering during casting

**Spell Access Progression (Suggested):**

- **Creation:** All cantrips, 3-4 standard, 1-2 advanced
- **Seasoned:** +2 standard, +1 advanced, possibly first master
- **Veteran:** Full standard/advanced access, 1-2 master
- **Heroic+:** All tiers available

**Balancing Magic:**

- Control starting Momentum
- Vary encounter length
- Include anti-magic elements
- Reward different approaches

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I cast at 0 Momentum?**
A: No, except cantrips (0 cost) or Will-based casting at negative Momentum

**Q: Do spells stack?**
A: Most do. Mage Armor doesn't stack with physical armor (use better). Multiple Shield spells don't stack (use highest).

**Q: Can I change my casting attribute?**
A: Yes, choose each time you cast

**Q: How many spells can I know?**
A: GM discretion. Suggested: All cantrips + progressive access to higher tiers

**Q: Can I counterspell?**
A: Yes, ready action to Dispel when enemy casts

**Q: What breaks Invisibility?**
A: GM discretion. Will version specifically maintains when attacking.

**Q: Can Heal bring back the dead?**
A: No, only dying/injured

**Q: Can Time Stop let me kill everyone?**
A: No, can't directly harm frozen creatures (position/buff only)

## For Players: Building a Caster

### Specialist Approach

- High in one attribute (Mind, Awareness, Will, or Presence)
- Always cast with that attribute
- Maximize that playstyle
- Iconic archetype (Wizard, Sorcerer, Cleric, Bard)

### Versatile Approach

- Balanced attributes
- Switch based on situation
- Adaptable magic
- Tactical flexibility

### Multiclass Concept

- Sorcery + martial skills
- Cast selectively for enhancement
- Gish/Eldritch Knight style
- Fewer spells, more versatility

### Key Decisions

1. **Primary Attribute:** Which casting style?
2. **Sorcery Investment:** How much?
3. **Spell Selection:** Combat/utility/social?
4. **Momentum Management:** Conservative or aggressive?
