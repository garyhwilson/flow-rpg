# Felisari

_The Feline Wanderers_

## Origin & Lore

Agile feline wanderers who dance through forests and cities alike, the Felisari embody curiosity made flesh. Their expressive nature and natural stealth make them excellent scouts, performers, and keepers of secrets both mundane and mystical.

## Physical Traits

- **Height:** 5'2" to 5'10"
- **Build:** Lithe with feline features
- **Lifespan:** 60-80 years
- **Vision:** Low-light vision

## Attribute Bonuses

+2 Grace, +1 Awareness, -1 Might

## Species Traits

### Cat's Grace
Gain advantage on checks to avoid or reduce fall damage. Your natural agility and instinctive body control help you land safely from dangerous heights.

### Low-Light Vision
See in dim light as if it were bright light. Your feline eyes gather available light efficiently, allowing you to navigate darkness others find challenging.

### Curiosity's Edge
Choose one at character creation: +1 to Stealth, Investigation, or Performance rolls. Your natural inquisitiveness manifests as specialized talent.

## Cultural Tendencies

Felisari rarely form large communities, preferring small family groups or integration into other societies. They value freedom, discovery, and the thrill of the unknown. Many become traveling performers or information brokers.

## Playing a Felisari

Your natural agility and senses make you excellent scouts and infiltrators. Choose your Curiosity's Edge based on your character concept. Embrace feline mannerisms and an inquisitive nature in roleplay.

**Narrative Themes:**
- Freedom and the refusal to be caged
- Curiosity driving discovery (and sometimes danger)
- Secrets collected and traded like currency
- Finding family in unexpected places

## Rank Progression

| Rank | Typical Role | Growth Focus |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------ |
| 1-2 | Street thief, alley lookout, local informant | Building networks and skills |
| 3-4 | Master burglar, guild enforcer, shadow in the night | Expanding influence |
| 5+ | Information broker, controls thieves' guilds across provinces | Trading in world-shaping secrets |

## Synergies

- Natural fit for Rogue archetypes at all ranks
- **Cat's Grace** synergizes with advanced acrobatic proficiencies at Rank 3+
- **Curiosity's Edge** makes them unmatched investigators at Rank 5+
- Low-light vision provides tactical advantage in dungeon and night scenarios
