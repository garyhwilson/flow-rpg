# Human

_The Adaptable Survivors_

## Origin & Lore

Found throughout Autumn Phoenix, humans are known for their adaptability, ambition, and dizzying diversity. Making up nearly two-thirds of the known population, they shape the world through determination and rapid innovation. Their brief lives drive them to achieve greatness within a single century.

## Physical Traits

- **Height:** 5'4" to 6'2"
- **Build:** Highly varied in appearance
- **Lifespan:** 70-90 years
- **Vision:** Normal

## Attribute Bonuses

+1 to any two different attributes (player's choice)

## Species Traits

### Versatile Heritage
Gain one additional utility proficiency at character creation, representing your exposure to diverse cultural practices and rapid skill acquisition.

### Resilient Will
Once per session, reroll any failed Will save. Your determination allows you to push through mental challenges that would break others.

### Rapid Learner
Reduce time to learn new proficiencies by 25% during downtime. Your adaptable mind grasps new concepts faster than other species.

## Cultural Tendencies

Humans build diverse societies across Autumn Phoenix, from the structured neighborhoods of Nix to the frontier settlements beyond. They value achievement, legacy, and progress, though definitions vary wildly between cultures.

## Playing a Human

Your adaptability is your strength. Choose attribute bonuses that support your character concept. Use Resilient Will for crucial moments and embrace humanity's diversity in your roleplay.

**Narrative Themes:**
- Ambition driving achievement within a mortal lifetime
- Building lasting legacies that outlive the individual
- Bridging gaps between longer-lived species
- Innovation born from necessity and limited time

## Rank Progression

| Rank | Typical Role | Growth Focus |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------ |
| 1-2 | Ambitious newcomer, adaptable survivor | Establishing skills and reputation |
| 3-4 | Rising leader, master of chosen path | Expanding influence and expertise |
| 5+ | Legendary figure, shapes provincial politics | Creating lasting impact on the world |

## Synergies

- **Versatile Heritage** provides increasing benefit as proficiencies multiply at higher ranks
- **Resilient Will** becomes crucial against powerful enemies at Rank 3+
- **Rapid Learner** allows unprecedented advancement at Rank 5+
- Natural fit for any archetype due to flexible attribute bonuses
