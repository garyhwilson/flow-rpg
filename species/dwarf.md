# Dwarf

_The Stoneborn Builders_

## Origin & Lore

Dwarves are the mountainfolk of Ashcrest, carved from stone and tempered by tradition. Master craftsmen and stalwart defenders, they value endurance, precision, and the strength found in community. Their societies run as deep as their mines.

## Physical Traits

- **Height:** 4'4" to 5'0"
- **Build:** Stocky and strong
- **Lifespan:** 150-200 years
- **Vision:** Normal

## Attribute Bonuses

+2 Might, +1 Mind, -1 Grace

## Species Traits

### Stoneborn Resilience
+3 to saves against poison and disease. Your hardy constitution shrugs off toxins that would fell other species.

### Enduring Frame
+2 to Vitality calculation. Your compact, dense physiology grants exceptional durability in combat and hardship.

### Crafted Precision
+1 bonus to all engineering and crafting rolls. Ancestral techniques and an instinctive understanding of materials guide your hands.

## Cultural Tendencies

Dwarven culture emphasizes clan loyalty, masterful craftsmanship, and the sacred nature of oaths. They build for permanence, whether forging weapons or friendships. Their connection to stone runs deeper than mere mining.

## Playing a Dwarf

Your durability makes you a natural defender. Your bonus Vitality helps you stay in fights longer. Use your crafting bonus for practical problem-solving and creating useful items during downtime.

**Narrative Themes:**
- Clan loyalty and ancestral obligations
- Craftsmanship as spiritual practice
- The weight of oaths sworn and kept
- Building things meant to last beyond a lifetime

## Rank Progression

| Rank | Typical Role | Growth Focus |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------ |
| 1-2 | Clan crafter, mountain guard, rune apprentice | Learning ancestral techniques |
| 3-4 | Master smith, stone singer, clan champion | Achieving craft mastery |
| 5+ | Forge legend, shapes metal with thought alone | Creating legendary works |

## Synergies

- **Enduring Frame** becomes more valuable as battles intensify at higher ranks
- **Crafted Precision** can create minor magic items at Rank 3+
- At Rank 5+, can forge legendary Phoenix-metal weapons
- Natural affinity for defensive combat and crafting archetypes
