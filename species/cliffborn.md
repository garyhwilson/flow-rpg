# Cliffborn

_The Mountain Scouts_

## Origin & Lore

Horned mountain scouts and climbers of Ashcrest's peaks, the Cliffborn navigate vertical worlds with ease. Their connection to wind and stone grants them supernatural balance and the ability to survive falls that would kill others.

## Physical Traits

- **Height:** 5'2" to 5'10"
- **Build:** Lean with small horns
- **Lifespan:** 70-100 years
- **Vision:** Normal

## Attribute Bonuses

+2 Grace, +1 Awareness, -1 Might

## Species Traits

### Sure-Footed
Gain advantage on Athletics checks to climb, leap, or balance. Your innate connection to vertical terrain makes you supremely confident on any surface.

### Keen Hearing
+2 to perception checks involving sound. Your ears are attuned to echoes and vibrations, allowing you to hear approaching danger and distant conversations.

### Wind-Bond
Once per day when falling 30+ feet, spend 1 Momentum as a reaction to reduce fall damage by half and land on your feet. The wind seems to catch you, slowing your descent.

## Cultural Tendencies

Cliffborn communities are built into mountainsides, with homes accessible only by climbing. They value courage, community support, and the wisdom found in high places. Many serve as guides or messengers between isolated settlements.

## Playing a Cliffborn

Your mobility options make you excellent at three-dimensional combat and exploration. Save your Wind-Bond for dramatic moments. Your keen hearing helps detect ambushes and eavesdrop effectively.

**Narrative Themes:**
- The call of high places and distant horizons
- Community bonds forged through mutual reliance
- Courage in the face of deadly terrain
- Messages carried between worlds that never meet

## Rank Progression

| Rank | Typical Role | Growth Focus |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------ |
| 1-2 | Mountain scout, cliff guide, messenger | Mastering terrain navigation |
| 3-4 | Elite ranger, mountain champion, wind dancer | Legendary climbing feats |
| 5+ | Legendary pathfinder, speaks with mountain spirits | Transcending physical limits |

## Synergies

- **Wind-Bond** becomes more valuable as falls get deadlier at high ranks
- Combines powerfully with Ranger archetypes for unmatched mobility at Rank 3+
- **Sure-Footed** allows impossible climbing feats at Rank 5+
- **Keen Hearing** provides early warning in combat and exploration
