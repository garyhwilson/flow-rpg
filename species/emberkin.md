# Emberkin

_The Fire-Touched_

## Origin & Lore

Born from volcanic regions or ancient fire rituals, Emberkin carry inner flames that manifest in times of stress. They embody the destructive and renewing aspects of fire, making them natural warriors and passionate artists who understand that destruction enables creation.

## Physical Traits

- **Height:** 5'0" to 6'0"
- **Build:** Warm skin tones with subtle flame-like markings
- **Lifespan:** 80-120 years
- **Vision:** Normal

## Attribute Bonuses

+2 Might, +1 Mind, -1 Grace

## Species Traits

### Smoldering Vitality
Resistance to fire damage (take half damage). Your inner flame provides natural protection against external heat and flames.

### Flame Wake
When reduced below half Vitality, automatically deal 1d6 fire damage to all adjacent enemies (once per combat). Your inner fire erupts when wounded, punishing those who stand too close.

### Ash-Wise
+1 bonus to all Lore checks involving fire, volcanoes, or ancient destruction. You possess an instinctive understanding of flame's history and power.

## Cultural Tendencies

Emberkin often gravitate toward professions involving fire—smiths, glassblowers, combat mages. They understand both creation and destruction, viewing the Phoenix Cycle as a personal truth written in their blood.

## Playing an Emberkin

Your fire resistance and Flame Wake make you dangerous when bloodied. Position yourself to maximize the area effect when you expect to take damage. Your lore bonus helps with fire-related puzzles and history.

**Narrative Themes:**
- Destruction as necessary precursor to renewal
- The Phoenix Cycle as personal philosophy
- Passion that creates and consumes in equal measure
- Finding beauty in ash and aftermath

## Rank Progression

| Rank | Typical Role | Growth Focus |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------ |
| 1-2 | Flame-touched warrior, apprentice forge worker | Controlling inner fire |
| 3-4 | Pyroclastic mage, volcanic oracle, phoenix speaker | Channeling destructive power |
| 5+ | Living flame incarnate, reshapes the land with fire | Becoming one with the cycle |

## Synergies

- **Flame Wake** triggers more frequently in larger battles at higher ranks
- Fire resistance negates many legendary threats at Rank 5+
- Phoenix Festival celebrations provide special bonuses during campaign events
- Natural affinity for martial and fire-based magical archetypes
